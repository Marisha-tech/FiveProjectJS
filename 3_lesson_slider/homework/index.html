Fern

The fern is a member of a group of vascular plants that reproduce by spores and lack seeds and flowers.

Monstera

Monstera - large tropical plants, vines; genus of the Aroid family.

Dieffenbachia

Dieffenbachia is a genus of evergreen plants of the Aroid family, common in the tropics of South and North America.

Ficus

Ficus is a genus of plants of the Mulberry family, in which it forms a monotypic tribe Ficus.
